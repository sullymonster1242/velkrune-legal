# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 25, 2026
**Last Updated:** May 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Velkrune ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Velkrune mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

If you have questions, contact us at: **velkrune@gmail.com**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide
- **Account information** — when you sign up: email address, password (stored only in encrypted hashed form by our authentication provider), and display name.
- **Profile information** — primary discipline (powerlifter, crossfit, etc.), bodyweight, training age, and bio. Optional and editable in Settings.
- **Training data** — exercises, sets, reps, weights, personal records, and morning check-in entries (sleep, soreness, well-being).
- **Nutrition data** — foods you log, custom foods you add, and portion sizes.

### Information collected automatically
- **Device information** — device type, operating system version, and app version. Used only to investigate crashes and compatibility issues.
- **Crash reports** — if the app crashes, we collect a stack trace and basic device info via Sentry (a third-party crash reporting service). No personally identifying information is included unless you have explicitly opted in.

### Information we do NOT collect
- Location data
- Contacts, calendar, or photo library access
- Browsing history outside the App
- Health data from HealthKit or Google Fit (these integrations are not active in this version)
- Microphone or camera input (these features are marked "Coming Soon" and do not function in this version)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide the core training, recovery, and nutrition logging features of the App
- Authenticate you and keep your data synchronized to your account
- Investigate crashes and improve App stability
- Respond to your support requests

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to anyone.

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only with the following service providers, and only to the extent necessary for them to provide their service to us:

- **Supabase, Inc.** — provides authentication and database hosting. Your email, password hash, and synchronized training data are stored on Supabase infrastructure. See https://supabase.com/privacy
- **Sentry (Functional Software, Inc.)** — receives anonymized crash reports. See https://sentry.io/privacy/
- **Apple Inc.** — App distribution, subscription processing (if applicable). See https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

We do not share your personal information with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties for marketing purposes.

We may disclose information if required by law (subpoena, court order, etc.), and will notify you of any such request unless legally prohibited from doing so.

---

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your account and training data for as long as your account is active.

When you delete your account (Settings → Delete Account inside the App), we permanently erase:
- All locally stored data on your device
- Your authentication record with our auth provider
- Your synchronized training and nutrition data

Anonymized crash reports may be retained by Sentry for up to 90 days per their default retention policy.

---

## 5. Your Rights

You can at any time:
- **Access and edit** your profile and training data inside the App
- **Export** your data via Settings → Export Data
- **Delete your account** via Settings → Delete Account. This is permanent and irreversible.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA, including the right to request a copy of your data, the right to correction, and the right to erasure. Contact us at the email above to exercise these rights.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Velkrune is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it.

---

## 7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including:
- HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
- Secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) for authentication tokens
- Password hashing handled by our authentication provider
- Access controls limiting employee access to user data

No security measure is perfect. If we become aware of a breach affecting your data, we will notify you promptly.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

If we change this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above and, for material changes, notify you in the App or by email.
